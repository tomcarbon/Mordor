The Black Land

Mount Doom

Orodruin, the Fire-mountain. Its furnaces burn eternal, forging shadow and flame. Here the One Ring was made, and here alone can it be unmade.

The Black Gate

Morannon, the impregnable fortress-gate. Iron teeth set in walls of stone and steel, guarded by thousands, breached by none who come uninvited.

Gorgoroth

The Plateau of Terror. A wasteland of ash and slag, scarred by the fires of Orodruin. No living green thing grows upon this blighted plain.

Cirith Ungol

The Spider's Pass. A treacherous stair winding through the Mountains of Shadow, guarded by She who dwells in darkness — Shelob the Great.
